# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 30, 2026

## Who we are

This Privacy Policy describes how **Pivotal Websites** (“we,” “us,” “our”) collects, uses, stores, and protects information when we operate our internal business systems, commercial outreach tools, and the Opportunity Operating System used to discover and pursue **business-to-business (B2B)** service opportunities—including commercial pressure washing and related local service offers.

This policy applies to:

- Operator and founder tools hosted on our platform
- Business discovery and lead qualification workflows
- Voice outreach through our telephony provider
- Internal mission planning and opportunity intelligence

We do **not** sell personal information. We use data to operate our business, pursue legitimate B2B opportunities, and improve our systems.

---

## Information we collect

### Business and professional contact information

When discovering or pursuing commercial opportunities, we may collect:

- Business name, address, phone number, and public website
- Professional role or title when publicly available or provided in conversation
- Email addresses for business contacts when used for B2B outreach
- Notes from sales calls, estimates, and follow-ups
- Outcomes such as quotes sent, jobs won or lost, and follow-up dates

We focus on **commercial** prospects, not residential consumer marketing lists.

### Discovery and intelligence data

Our systems may process publicly available or licensed business information from sources such as:

- Google Maps / Google Places and related location APIs
- Public business listings and websites
- File-based or connector-fed business signals ingested into our operating system

We record **provenance** (where data came from) for auditability.

### Operator and founder system data

When founders or operators use our platform, we may store:

- Account name and email for authentication
- Mission definitions, strategy notes, and approval decisions
- Founder intent and conversation context used by internal planning tools (not for autonomous outreach without approval)
- Engineering and system health metadata

### Voice call data

When click-to-call or recorded outreach is used, our telephony provider (Twilio) may process:

- Phone numbers dialed and call metadata
- Call recordings and transcripts when recording is enabled
- Connection and delivery status

Twilio processes this data under its own privacy and security terms.

### Technical data

We may collect standard server logs, device/browser information, and cookies needed for authentication and security.

---

## How we use information

We use information to:

- Identify and prioritize legitimate B2B commercial opportunities
- Operate sales queues, follow-ups, and founder daily workflows
- Place and record business development phone calls
- Plan missions, rank opportunities, and prepare outreach **subject to founder approval**
- Maintain CRM-style records of outreach attempts and outcomes
- Secure, debug, and improve our systems
- Comply with law and enforce our terms

We do **not** use this platform for autonomous bulk consumer messaging. Outreach requires explicit founder or operator approval under current operating policy.

## Legal bases (summary)

Depending on context, we rely on:

- **Legitimate business interests** in B2B prospecting and operating our sales systems
- **Contract** where we provide services to customers or partners
- **Consent** where required (for example optional marketing communications)
- **Legal obligation** where applicable

---

## Sharing and processors

We share information only as needed to operate the service, including with:

- **Hosting and storage providers** (for example Vercel and associated blob storage)
- **Google Cloud / Maps Platform** for business discovery APIs
- **Twilio** for voice calling and recordings
- **Email providers** when outbound email is enabled (future)
- **Payment processors** (Stripe) for website product billing where applicable

We require service providers to protect data and use it only for the services they provide to us.

We do not sell personal information.

---

## Retention

We retain business and operational records as long as needed for:

- Active sales follow-up and customer relationships
- Legal, tax, and dispute resolution requirements
- System audit and improvement

Founder conversation and mission planning data is retained in our runtime systems according to operational need and can be archived when missions close.

---

## Security

We use access controls, authenticated operator sessions, environment-based secrets, and least-privilege practices. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we work to reduce risk commensurate with the sensitivity of B2B operational data.

---

## Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain processing of personal information.

**Business contacts** who receive outreach may:

- Ask not to be contacted again (we maintain suppression lists for opt-out requests)
- Request correction of inaccurate business contact details
- Ask what information we hold about their business contact record

**Operators** can request account changes through the platform owner.

To submit a privacy request, contact: **privacy@pivotalwebsites.com**

We will respond within a reasonable time and verify requests where appropriate.
